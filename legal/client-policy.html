1. Age Representation & Parental Responsibility

⚠ No Age Verification — You Represent Your Age

By hiring me, making a payment, or purchasing any product, you represent and warrant that you are 18 years of age or older. I do not independently verify age. If you are a minor, you must have your parent or legal guardian engage me directly.

Legal Effect of Misrepresentation

  • ⚖️Under Section 11 of the Indian Contract Act, 1872, a minor cannot enter into a valid contract. Any agreement with a minor is void ab initio.
  • ⚠️If you are a minor and you have falsely represented yourself as 18+, you have committed fraud and misrepresentation. I rely on your representation as a material term of our agreement.
  • 💰Under Section 65 of the Contract Act, if a contract is void, any person who has received a benefit must restore it. Since digital services and custom work cannot be "un-received," you (or your guardian) remain liable for the full payment as compensation for unjust enrichment.
  • 👨‍👩‍👧Your parent or guardian shall be pursued for payment under the law of restitution, negligence (failing to secure their payment instruments), and tort of deceit.

Parental Responsibility for Payment Instruments

🔴 Important: If your minor child uses your credit/debit card, UPI ID, net banking, or any other payment method to engage my services or purchase my products, you are fully liable for the payment. You have failed in your duty to secure your financial instruments. I will pursue the account holder (the person whose payment method was used) as the de facto client under the principle of agency by estoppel and ratification.

Refunds for Unauthorized Minor Purchases

🕐 Limited Window — Store Products & Subscriptions Only
  • ⏱️If an unauthorized purchase was made by a minor using your payment method, you must notify me within 7 days of the charge appearing on your bank statement.
  • 📥If notified within 7 days and the digital product (script, subscription access, downloadable file) has not been downloaded, accessed, or used, I will refund 80% of the amount (20% processing fee retained).
  • 🚫If the product has been downloaded, accessed, or used, no refund will be provided. Digital products cannot be returned. The account holder is solely responsible for securing their payment instruments.
  • ⚡For custom service projects, no refunds are provided once any milestone has been delivered and approved, regardless of the client's age representation. The adult whose payment method was used remains fully liable.
Why this policy exists: Under the Indian Contract Act 1872, agreements with minors are void. However, I am not a verification agency. I rely on your representation. This policy protects both parties by shifting legal responsibility to the person who controls the payment method — where it belongs. If you do not want your minor child making purchases, secure your UPI apps and cards.
