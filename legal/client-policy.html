1. Minor Clients

⚠ Strict Policy — No Exceptions

Clients under the age of 18 cannot engage in any paid service without verified parental or legal guardian consent. Work will not begin until this process is complete.

Verification Process

  • 📋Minor must provide a valid Aadhaar card as identity proof.
  • 📞I will directly contact the parent/guardian via WhatsApp or call before any work begins.
  • ✍️Parent/guardian must sign a written consent agreement covering project scope and payment terms.
  • 💳All payments must come from the parent's UPI/bank account, not the minor.
  • 🚫If the parent is unreachable or unwilling to sign, the project will be declined regardless of payment offered.
Why this policy exists: Under the Indian Contract Act 1872, agreements with minors are void. This process protects both parties and ensures every project has full legal standing.

3. Refund Policy

🔒 Non-Refundable Once Approved

Once a milestone is delivered and approved by the client (verbally, via WhatsApp, or in writing), that payment is final and non-refundable. Approval includes any message such as "ok", "looks good", "done", or "proceed".

Refund Eligibility

✅ Eligible for Refund
  • ✔Work not started on a paid milestone due to my unavailability.
  • ✔Project cancelled by me before any milestone delivery.
  • ✔Store product is completely non-functional and I am unable to fix it within 7 days.
❌ Not Eligible for Refund
  • ✖Milestone delivered and approved by the client.
  • ✖Client changed their mind about the project after work began.
  • ✖Client requested features outside the original agreed scope.
  • ✖Store products that have been downloaded and used.
  • ✖Subscription months already consumed.
