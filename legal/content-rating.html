Content Rating Guide

Information for players and parents about content in our games

Last Updated: December 22, 2025

At Graphicy, we create games with a wide audience in mind. This guide provides clear information about the content you can expect in our games, helping you make informed choices.

Our General Rating

Our games are typically designed to be suitable for Everyone (E) or Everyone 10+ (E10+). We avoid including realistic violence, strong language, or adult themes.

E10+

Everyone 10+ (E10+)

Content is generally suitable for ages 10 and up. May contain more cartoon, fantasy or mild violence, mild language, and/or minimal suggestive themes. This is the most common rating for our action or adventure games.

Content Descriptors

Based on industry-standard guidelines (like ESRB/IARC), our games may include the following types of content:

Fantasy Violence

Characters may engage in conflict in a cartoonish, exaggerated, or unrealistic manner. There is no blood, gore, or realistic consequences. Defeated enemies may disappear or be knocked out.

Mild Language

Very infrequent use of mild, non-sexual expletives or name-calling (e.g., "jerk," "darn").

Comic Mischief

Scenarios may include slapstick humor or non-dangerous, comedic pranks.

Interactive Elements

This section details features beyond the game's story content that users should be aware of.

Users Interact

Some of our games feature multiplayer modes where players can communicate through pre-selected phrases or emotes. We do not support open text or voice chat. Players can report others for inappropriate use of these tools.

In-Game Purchases (Digital Storefront)

The game includes the ability to purchase virtual items with real money. This includes optional cosmetic items, in-game currency, or season passes. All purchases are optional and are not required to complete the game.

Parental Guidance Recommended: You can disable in-app purchases through your device's settings.

Unrestricted Internet

The game can connect to the internet to display third-party advertisements (via Google AdMob) and for general functionality updates. Ad content is not controlled by Graphicy.

Our Commitment

We strive to create fun, engaging experiences that are appropriate for their intended audience. Our goal is to be transparent so players and parents know what to expect.

Note: This guide is provided for informational purposes. The official age rating for each game is determined by digital storefronts (like Google Play Store or Amazon Appstore) during the submission process and will be displayed on the game's store page.
