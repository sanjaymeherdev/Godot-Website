Common questions

Is Advanced and Pro really one-time? No subscription?
Yes. You pay once and keep access to those generators permanently. No recurring charge, no expiry. Only Premium is monthly because it includes the GodotConnect P2P server slot which has ongoing infrastructure cost.
What is the GodotConnect P2P slot in Premium?
It's a live GodotConnect game slot — up to 10 concurrent players, private virtual IPs so real IPs are never exposed, host migration, lobby, chat, and auth all included. The same service sold separately at GodotConnect — bundled into Premium at no extra cost.
Can I use AI instead of buying this?
You can try. ChatGPT generates Godot 3 syntax roughly half the time, doesn't know your node hierarchy, and the @rpc patterns it produces usually have wrong authority settings. Every generator here is Godot 4 tested on a real multiplayer project. The free tier costs nothing — try it first and see the difference.
How do I access the generators after buying?
Contact via YouTube @ChampGaming after payment. You'll receive login credentials for the pro generators. Payment via UPI or bank transfer (India) — international options available on request.
What if I start on Advanced and want to upgrade to Pro?
You pay the difference — ₹600 / $7 to go from Advanced to Pro. Contact via YouTube and it gets sorted. No need to pay full price again.
What does "free store addons" in Premium mean exactly?
Selected scripts and assets from the SanjayAIDev store are made available to Premium members at no charge. These rotate and expand over time — not every single item in the store, but a curated set of useful addons relevant to what you're building.
