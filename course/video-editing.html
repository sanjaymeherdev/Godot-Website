Before you ask

Quick answers.

Do I need to buy any software?

No. Every tool taught — CapCut, Filmora, and the AI tools — is free. After Effects basics are shown live; paid software is always optional, never required to complete the course.

Will this teach me to make AI-generated videos?

No. This course does not cover AI movies, text-to-video, or full AI-generated videos. The AI portion is limited to free tools for short B-roll clips and scenes — used to support your editing, not replace it.

What if I'm a complete beginner?

That's fine — the course is built for a mixed group, from first-timers to people with some editing experience. Sessions are live, so you can ask questions at your own pace.

Is there a certificate?

No certificate is issued. The focus here is real, usable skill — not a PDF to add to your profile.

What language are the sessions in?
