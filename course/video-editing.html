Before you ask

Quick answers.

Do I need to buy any software?

No. Every tool taught — CapCut, Filmora, and the AI tools — is free. Paid software is always optional, never required to complete the course.

Is this an AI-only course?

No. This is not a shortcut course that replaces editing with AI prompts. You learn real, hands-on video editing in CapCut and Filmora, and AI video creation as one more skill alongside it — not instead of it.

What if I'm a complete beginner?

That's fine — the course is built for a mixed group, from first-timers to people with some editing experience, and goes from beginner to advanced over the 4 weeks.

What happens on Sundays?

No new lessons on Sundays — that day is kept free for live doubt-clearing on everything covered that week.

Is there a certificate?

No certificate is issued. The focus here is real, usable skill — not a PDF to add to your profile.

What language are the sessions in?

Hindi, conducted live over Google Meet.
