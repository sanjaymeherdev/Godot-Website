The Full Pipeline

Six modules — from scene layout to final compositing. Every step is repeatable, controllable, and AI‑assisted.

01

Scene Layout

Build props with proper names & bounds. AI arranges them via JSON — position, rotation, scale.

02

Texturing

Create clean UV maps. AI generates textures that fit your UV perfectly — no stretching, no seams.

03

Animation

Model a character → Mixamo auto‑rig → download animations. AI sequences them based on your story.

04

Procedural & Simulation

AI helps build geometry nodes, particles, destruction — and scripts Blender's simulation engines.

05

Compositing

AI builds node trees — color grading, effects, motion graphics. Shape keys & geo nodes for animation.

06

Final Project

Combine everything into a short film — consistent characters, lighting, and style. Portfolio ready.
