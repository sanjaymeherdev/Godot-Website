Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any coding experience?

No. The course is built for absolute beginners. You'll learn everything from the ground up — no prior coding knowledge required.

What will I be able to build after this course?

You'll be able to build AI chat apps, booking systems, client dashboards, internal HR tools, and more — all self-hosted, all yours.

Can I earn money from what I build?

Yes. The Earn Track covers three paths: use it for your own business, sell it to other businesses, or offer it as a service. All three are real, practical ways to make money.

What if I'm a complete beginner?

That's exactly who this is for. You'll go from zero to building real, working web apps in 4 weeks.

Do I need to buy any software?

No. All tools we use are free or have generous free tiers — Supabase, n8n, Render, Vercel, and more.

What language are the sessions in?

Hindi, conducted live over Google Meet.
