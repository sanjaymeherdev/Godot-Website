Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Will this course help me get freelance work?

Yes. The Freelancer Business Track is designed specifically to help you find clients, price your work, and handle revisions professionally. You'll learn real-world client skills, not just editing.

Do I need a portfolio before joining?

No. You'll build projects during the course that you can use in your portfolio. The freelancer module also covers how to create a portfolio that gets you hired.

What if I have no experience with clients?

That's fine. The freelancer module starts from absolute basics – how to find clients, what to say, how to charge, and how to handle the first few projects.

Will you teach how to handle difficult clients or revisions?

Yes. We cover revision management, setting boundaries, pricing for revisions, and delivering work that keeps clients happy without burning you out.

Do I need to buy any software?

No. Every tool taught – CapCut, Filmora, and the AI tools – is free. Paid software is always optional, never required.

Is this an AI-only course?

No. You learn real, hands-on editing. AI is taught as an additional skill alongside it – not instead of it.

What language are sessions in?

Hindi, conducted live over Google Meet.
