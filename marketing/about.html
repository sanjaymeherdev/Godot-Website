About Sanjay Meher

Founder of Graphicy — 7+ years of turning creative visions into reality

👋 My Story

From a young age, I was fascinated with technology and creativity. This passion led me to learn video editing, which quickly turned into a profession. I started freelancing, earning extra money in my free time while pursuing my studies and a full-time job.

The experience encouraged me to learn other related skills like Graphic Design, WordPress Website Design, Digital Marketing, and AI. With an experience of over 7 years in Video Editing & Graphic Design and 2 years in Digital Marketing, I've worked for multiple clients across diverse niches.

"I started my agency Graphicy in 2023 to help businesses grow through creative excellence and digital marketing — while also teaching others the skills I've mastered."

💼 What I Do

I help businesses — from small local shops to growing brands — stand out with professional video editing, graphic design, and digital marketing strategies. Every project is custom-tailored to your needs, with transparent pricing and no hidden fees.
