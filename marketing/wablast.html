FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is this different from unofficial WhatsApp blasters? ›
Yes — completely. Unofficial tools work by automating the regular WhatsApp app, which violates Meta's terms and gets numbers banned regularly. WaBlast Pro uses the official WhatsApp Business API, which is Meta-approved and the only legally compliant way to send bulk messages.
Do I need a WhatsApp Business Account already? ›
You need a Meta Business account and a WhatsApp Business number set up in Meta's system. We'll walk you through generating a System User access token in Meta Business Manager and connecting it to WaBlast — most people are set up within a few minutes.
Why do I need Meta-approved templates? ›
WhatsApp's API requires all outbound bulk messages to use pre-approved templates. This prevents spam at scale. You can't send free-form messages in bulk — only to users who messaged you first within 24 hours. WaBlast's template builder makes creating and submitting templates easy.
How many messages can I send per day? ›
Meta controls this via tiers. New numbers start at 250 messages/day (Tier 1). As your number builds history and maintains a good quality rating, Meta automatically increases this to 1,000 → 10,000 → 100,000/day. WaBlast lets you set your own hourly and daily limits so you stay comfortably within your current tier.
How do I get access to WaBlast Pro? ›
Depends on the plan. "Use My Number" needs no account at all — just send us your message and contact list and we handle it. Every other plan (own number, full dashboard, API) is set up directly by our team — message us on WhatsApp or email and we'll get you going, usually the same day.
Is my data safe? ›
All data is stored encrypted on Supabase with row-level security. Your WhatsApp access tokens are encrypted at rest. We never share or sell your data. See the Privacy Policy for full details.
Can I trigger messages from my own app or automation tool? ›
Yes — if your account has API access enabled, you can generate API keys with scoped permissions and call WaBlast's API from tools like n8n, Zapier, or your own backend to send messages and trigger campaigns.
