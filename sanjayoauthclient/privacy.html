SanjayOauthClient – Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026 | Last updated: June 1, 2026

SanjayOauthClient ("we", "our", or "us") operates a simple authentication service that allows users to sign in using their Google account. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our authentication service.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google through SanjayOauthClient, we receive only the information that you consent to share. Specifically, we request and access:

  • Email address – used as your unique identifier and to communicate with you (if needed).
  • Name – to personalize your experience (e.g., displaying "Welcome, [Your Name]").
  • Profile picture URL – to optionally show your avatar inside the application.
  • Google ID (sub) – a unique, non-public identifier that helps us recognize returning users.

What we do NOT collect: We never access your Google Drive, Gmail, Calendar, Contacts, YouTube data, location, phone number, or any other sensitive or restricted scopes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect only for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity and grant you access to protected areas of our application.
  • To display your name and email address inside your user session (so you know which account is signed in).
  • To improve and debug the authentication flow (no automated decision-making or profiling).

We do not use your email for marketing, newsletters, or sharing with third parties. We do not build any behavioral profiles.

3. Data Storage and Retention

SanjayOauthClient does not run its own database for storing your personal information by default. Your authentication is token-based:

  • When you sign in, Google issues an ID token. We verify the token and create a temporary session (usually stored in browser LocalStorage or a session cookie).
  • We do not permanently store your email, name, or profile picture on our servers unless you voluntarily save preferences (e.g., dark mode setting).
  • If you log out, your session is destroyed. No background retention occurs.

In cases where we need to store minimal data (like a user ID to recognize returning visitors), we use hashed or pseudonymous identifiers.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties. The only exception is if required by law (e.g., valid court order). We do not use analytics services that track you across websites.

5. Your Google Account & OAuth Consent

SanjayOauthClient uses Google OAuth 2.0 and OpenID Connect. When you click "Sign in with Google", you will see a consent screen that lists the exact scopes we request: email, profile, and openid. You may revoke access at any time by visiting your Google Account's "Third-party apps with account access" page.

6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the information transmitted during authentication, including HTTPS encryption and token validation. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
