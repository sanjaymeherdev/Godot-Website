Automation Hub – Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 25, 2026 | Last updated: June 25, 2026

Automation Hub ("we", "our", or "us") operates a workflow automation platform built on n8n that connects to your Google Workspace services. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our platform.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. All data processed through workflows is handled ephemerally – meaning it is not stored beyond workflow execution.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google and use Automation Hub, we access the following types of information:

🔐 User Identity (Authentication)

  • Email address – used as your unique identifier and to assign ownership of workflows
  • Name – to personalize your dashboard and workflow assignments
  • Profile picture URL – to display your avatar in the application
  • Google ID (sub) – a unique, non-public identifier that helps us recognize returning users

📧 Gmail Data

  • Email content and metadata – only for emails you explicitly select as workflow triggers
  • Labels and folders – used to organize emails according to your automation rules
  • Drafts and sent messages – created through automated replies and email-sending workflows
🔹 Scope: gmail.modify – Read, send, and organize emails

📊 Google Sheets Data

  • Spreadsheet content – only for sheets you explicitly connect to workflows
  • Cell values and formulas – read/written during data sync and report generation
  • Spreadsheet metadata – to identify and manage connected sheets
🔹 Scope: spreadsheets – Read and write spreadsheet data

📁 Google Drive Data

  • File content – only for files/folders you explicitly include in automation
  • File metadata – name, type, size, and folder location for organization
  • Folder structure – used to organize and share files based on workflow rules
🔹 Scope: drive – Full access to files and folders you select for automation

📅 Calendar & Meet Data

  • Event details – only for events created or modified by your workflows
  • Calendar availability – checked to avoid double-booking when scheduling
  • Attendee information – used to send invites and manage RSVPs
  • Meet links – automatically generated for video meetings
🔹 Scope: calendar + calendar.events – Manage calendar events and Meet links

⚙️ Apps Script Data

  • Script content – your custom code stored in Google Apps Script projects
  • Execution logs – to debug and troubleshoot script runs
  • API calls – scripts may call external APIs as defined by you
🔹 Scope: script.external_request, script.scriptapp, script.send_mail – Run custom scripts and send emails

📊 Workflow Metadata

  • Workflow definitions – your n8n workflow configurations and logic
  • Execution logs – for debugging and auditing workflow runs
  • Usage statistics – to monitor performance and improve the platform
✅ Data Commitment: All data processed through Automation Hub is ephemeral – it exists only during workflow execution and is not stored, shared, or sold. We do not retain emails, files, calendar events, or spreadsheet content after workflows complete.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected only for the following purposes:

  • Authentication – to verify your identity and grant access to the platform
  • Workflow execution – to run your automation workflows as configured
  • Personalization – to display your name, email, and avatar in the dashboard
  • Debugging & Support – to troubleshoot workflow failures and improve performance
  • Auditing – to track workflow ownership and execution history

We do not use your data for marketing, advertising, or any purpose unrelated to providing the automation service.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Automation Hub follows strict data retention practices:

  • Ephemeral Processing: Emails, file content, and calendar data are processed in-memory during workflow execution and immediately discarded afterward
  • Authentication Tokens: OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored securely to maintain your session and run workflows
  • Workflow Definitions: Your workflow configurations are stored in our database to persist your automations
  • Execution Logs: Logs are retained for debugging purposes and are automatically purged after 30 days
  • User Preferences: UI settings (e.g., dark mode, dashboard layout) are stored as user preferences

You may delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting our support team.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties. Exceptions include:

  • Google APIs – we communicate with Google's services to execute your workflows (emails, sheets, drive, calendar, apps script)
  • Legal Requirements – if required by law or valid court order
  • Service Providers – we may use third-party hosting providers who are bound by strict confidentiality agreements

We do not use analytics services that track you across websites or build behavioral profiles.

5. Your Google Account & OAuth Consent

Automation Hub uses Google OAuth 2.0 to access your Workspace services. When you sign in, you will see a consent screen listing the following scopes:

📧 gmail.modify
📊 spreadsheets
📁 drive
📅 calendar
📅 calendar.events
⚙️ script.external_request
⚙️ script.scriptapp
⚙️ script.send_mail
👤 userinfo.email
👤 userinfo.profile
🔐 openid

You may revoke Automation Hub's access at any time by visiting your Google Account → Security → Third-party apps with account access.

6. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Token Security: OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored with strict access controls
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to system logs and workflows
  • No Permanent Storage: Ephemeral data processing minimizes data exposure

While we follow industry best practices, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: You may correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Revocation: You may revoke OAuth access at any time from your Google Account
  • Export: You may export your workflow definitions for backup

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, contact us and we will delete it.
