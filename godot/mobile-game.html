The Story

No Documents.
No Identity.
No Way Out.

A fake agency promised isolated people high-paid jobs abroad. The real goal: organ harvesting. Police raided the arrival airport — but this is a corrupt country. Victims have nothing: no documents, no money, no identity. An airport guard points you to Rico — a smuggler who lives outside the law but has his own code. Work for him, earn his trust, and maybe find a way out.

Every mission is generated by AI — desperate, morally complex, survival-driven. Earn trust. Build a reputation. Find a way out.
