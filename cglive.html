🎯 What is CGLive?

CGLive is a feature-rich Android live streaming application that enables seamless multi-platform broadcasting directly from your mobile device. Stream simultaneously to YouTube, Facebook, and Twitch with a single tap.

⚡ Powered By

RootEncoder (pedroSG94) MediaProjection API OpenGL ES RTMP Protocol WebSocket Hardware Encoding

✨ Key Features

🎮
Game Screen Capture

Stream your gameplay using Android's MediaProjection API with hardware-accelerated encoding. Capture smooth 60fps gameplay with minimal performance impact.

📷
Camera Picture-in-Picture

Floating camera overlay that stays on top of your gameplay. Orientation-aware rendering with adjustable size and position.

🎞️
GIF Overlay Support

Add animated GIF banners directly in the GL pipeline. Perfect for logos, alerts, or donation goals. Composite seamlessly with your stream.

🖼️
Stream Scenes

Professional looking Starting, BRB (Be Right Back), and Ending scenes. Support for images, videos, and animated GIFs.

📊
Live Dashboard

Real-time chat, viewer count, likes, donations, and subscriptions from YouTube, Twitch, and Facebook - all in one place.

🎚️
Floating Stream Controls

Control your stream from anywhere with a floating bubble overlay. Toggle mic, camera, adjust bitrate, or activate BRB mode without leaving your app.

🔴
Multi-destination RTMP

Stream to up to 4 platforms simultaneously. Custom RTMP URLs supported for custom streaming servers.

💬
StreamElements Integration

WebSocket connection for StreamElements alerts. Get real-time notifications for follows, subs, tips, and more.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is CGLive completely free?
Yes, CGLive is completely free to use with no hidden costs or in-app purchases. You only need your own stream keys from YouTube, Facebook, or Twitch (which are free to obtain).
Can I stream to multiple platforms at once?
Absolutely! CGLive supports simultaneous streaming to YouTube, Facebook, Twitch, and custom RTMP servers. You can stream to up to 4 platforms at the same time.
Does it affect game performance?
CGLive uses hardware-accelerated encoding and is optimized for minimal performance impact. On modern devices, you can expect 60fps streaming with minimal battery drain.
Can I use my own overlays?
Yes! You can add your own images, GIFs, and videos as overlays. The app supports custom BRB screens, starting soon scenes, and animated GIF banners.
Do I need a powerful phone?
CGLive runs on any Android 8.0+ device, but for smooth 1080p60 streaming, we recommend devices with at least 4GB RAM and modern processors (Snapdragon 700 series or equivalent).
How do I get stream keys?
Each platform provides stream keys in your creator dashboard. YouTube: Creator Studio → Go Live → Stream Key. Facebook: Creator Studio → Live Dashboard. Twitch: Creator Dashboard → Stream Key.
