FAQ

Common Questions

How does "pay after it works" actually work?

I build first, you test in your own environment. Only once you confirm it does what you need, you pay. If it doesn't work as scoped, we keep fixing until it does — no payment until then.

How fast is delivery?

Most standard services — video editing, graphic design, AI automation — are 3-7 days. Larger builds like full websites typically take 7-14 days. I'll give you an exact timeline before starting.

Do I need to be technical?

Not at all. You describe the problem in plain language. I handle everything and explain what I built in plain terms.

What if I need changes after delivery?

3 free revisions are included after every delivery. Changes within the original scope are free. New features outside scope are discussed and priced separately.

Will I own the final work?

Yes. Everything I build for you is yours — the files, the source code, the designs. No licensing, no ongoing fees from me.

I'm not sure what I need — can I still message you?

That's the best time to message. Describe the problem, not the solution — and I'll tell you exactly what makes sense and what it costs.
