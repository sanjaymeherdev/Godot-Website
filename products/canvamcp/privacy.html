01Overview

AIDesignApp is a Canva app that lets you connect your own AI endpoints to the Canva editor using the Model Context Protocol (MCP). This policy explains what information AIDesignApp accesses, how it is used, and the choices you have. AIDesignApp is operated by Sanjay Meher.

02Data we collect

AIDesignApp collects the minimum information needed to operate inside Canva:

  • Canva account information — basic profile details (such as your name and user ID) provided by Canva when you authorize the app, used to identify your session.
  • Endpoint configuration — the URL and any credentials you enter to connect your own AI endpoint. Credentials are stored only to make requests on your behalf and are not used for any other purpose.
  • Design content you choose to send — text, prompts, images, or selected canvas elements that you actively submit to your configured AI endpoint through the app.
  • Basic usage data — technical logs (such as request timestamps and error codes) used to keep the app reliable and to diagnose issues.

AIDesignApp does not access your Canva designs, files, or account beyond what Canva's permission system grants for the app's stated functionality.

03Your AI endpoint

AIDesignApp is designed to be a bridge, not a data destination. When you send content to an AI endpoint you have configured, that content travels from Canva to the endpoint you specified, and the endpoint's response is returned to your canvas. We do not route your prompts or design content through any AI model of our own choosing, and we do not use that content to train any model.

Because the endpoint is one you control, its own privacy and data-handling practices apply once your content reaches it. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any AI service you connect.

04How we use data

  • To operate the core function of the app: relaying requests between Canva and your configured endpoint.
  • To authenticate you with Canva and remember your endpoint configuration between sessions.
  • To maintain, secure, and improve the app, including diagnosing and fixing errors.
  • To respond to support requests you send us.

05Sharing & disclosure

We do not sell your data. We do not share your Canva account information, endpoint credentials, or design content with third parties, except:

  • With the AI endpoint you personally configure, as a direct result of using the app.
  • With service providers who help operate the app's infrastructure (such as hosting), under obligations to protect your data.
  • Where required by law, regulation, or a valid legal process.

06Retention

Endpoint configuration is retained for as long as your account is connected to AIDesignApp, so the app can function between sessions. Design content and prompts sent to your endpoint are relayed in real time and are not stored by AIDesignApp beyond what is technically necessary to complete the request. You can disconnect your endpoint or uninstall the app at any time to remove stored configuration.

07Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information AIDesignApp handles, including encrypted transport for requests between Canva, AIDesignApp, and your endpoint. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

08Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you, or to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

09Children's privacy

AIDesignApp is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required by your local law), and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of AIDesignApp after changes take effect means you accept the revised policy.
