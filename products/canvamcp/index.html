Signal Path
Setup

Three steps from canvas to custom model.

01

Point it at your endpoint

Add the URL of any MCP-compatible AI service — your own model, a hosted one, or an internal tool.

02

Open the panel in Canva

AIDesignApp appears in the Canva sidebar. Select an element, a prompt, or a whole page as context.

03

Generate in place

Your endpoint's response — text, image, or layout — lands directly on the canvas, ready to adjust.

Why it's different

Built to be a bridge, not a walled garden.

Bring your own model

No lock-in to one AI provider. Connect anything that speaks MCP — proprietary, open-source, or fine-tuned in house.

Runs where you work

No context switching. Prompts, references, and results all stay inside the Canva editor.

You control the data path

Requests go straight from Canva to the endpoint you configured — not through a third-party model AIDesignApp picks for you.

Built for iteration

Re-run a prompt against a design element in seconds, and keep refining without leaving the page you're working on.
