No. Pricing is flat per tool for your team. Adding a client account doesn't push you into a new tier — that's the entire point of this stack.

Yes. Every tool in the stack works standalone. Most agencies start with GMB Rank Tracker or LeadFlow and add the rest once the first one proves itself.
