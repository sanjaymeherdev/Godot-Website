What happens automatically

From "interested" to a confirmed visit — untouched by hand.

One buyer fills a short form. Here's everything that happens next, in order.

STEP 01

Buyer submits a short interest form

Budget, preferred locality, and a visit date — takes them under a minute.

STEP 02

Your inventory gets matched instantly

The system checks their answers against your live property list and shortlists the actual fits — not everything you have.

STEP 03

A personalized property deck is built

Photos, price, and locality for each matched property, assembled into a clean deck — automatically, every time.

STEP 04

The site visit is booked and confirmed

A slot goes straight onto the assigned agent's calendar, with a video call link included for buyers who want a virtual walkthrough first.

STEP 05

The buyer gets one email with everything

Their property deck, the visit date and time, the meeting link, and a short form for anything else you need from them before the visit.

STEP 06

Your lead sheet updates itself

Status, matched properties, and visit time — logged, so you always know where every buyer stands without asking anyone.
