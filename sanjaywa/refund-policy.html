Refund & Cancellation Policy

Graphicy Media · AutoSend Platform
Effective Date: 10 May 2026

This policy governs the purchase, use, and refund of credits on the AutoSend platform. Please read this policy carefully before purchasing credits.

1. Nature of Credits

AutoSend operates on a pay-per-use credit system. Credits are prepaid digital units used to send WhatsApp messages through the Platform. Each credit purchase includes the applicable Meta WhatsApp Business API conversation cost for the message category and the AutoSend platform usage fee for infrastructure, delivery, and support.

Credits have no monetary exchange value and cannot be transferred, exchanged, or redeemed for cash.

2. No Refund on Used Credits

Credits that have been consumed (i.e., used to send messages) are strictly non-refundable. This includes messages that were delivered, failed to deliver, or were skipped, as the API cost and platform fee are incurred at the point of send attempt.

3. Refund Eligibility for Unused Credits

Refunds for unused credits may be considered only under the following conditions:

  • A written refund request is submitted to Graphicyin@gmail.com within 7 days of the credit purchase date
  • The credits remain fully unused at the time of the request
  • The purchase was made in error or there was a proven technical failure in credit delivery (credits charged but not added to account)
Note: Refund requests submitted after 7 days of purchase, or for credits that have been partially or fully used, will not be eligible for refund under any circumstances.

4. Technical Failure Refunds

If your payment was successfully charged by Razorpay but credits were not added to your AutoSend account:

  • Contact us within 48 hours at Graphicyin@gmail.com with your Razorpay transaction ID
  • We will investigate and rectify the issue within 3 business days
  • If the issue cannot be resolved technically, a full refund of the failed transaction will be processed
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment source via Razorpay within 5–7 business days

5. Account Suspension or Termination

  • If your account is suspended or terminated due to a violation of our Terms of Service or Acceptable Use Policy, no refund will be issued for any unused credits
  • If your account is terminated at our discretion for reasons not related to policy violations, unused credits may be refunded on a case-by-case basis at our sole discretion

6. Razorpay Payment Disputes

If you initiate a chargeback or payment dispute through your bank or Razorpay without first contacting us, we reserve the right to immediately suspend your account pending investigation, deduct the disputed credit amount from your account balance, and permanently ban the account if the dispute is found to be fraudulent.

We strongly encourage resolving any billing concerns directly with us before raising a payment dispute.
