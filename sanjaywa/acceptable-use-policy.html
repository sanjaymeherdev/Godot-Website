Acceptable Use Policy

Graphicy Media · AutoSend Platform
Effective Date: 10 May 2026
This policy is critical. Graphicy Media is bound by Meta's WhatsApp Business Platform policies. Violations by any client on our platform can result in our entire Meta Business Account being restricted. We take AUP violations extremely seriously and enforce them without exception.

1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") defines what constitutes acceptable and unacceptable use of the AutoSend platform. It exists to protect all users of the platform, their recipients, and to ensure Graphicy Media's continued compliance with Meta's WhatsApp Business Policy, Indian law, and global anti-spam standards. This AUP must be read alongside our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

2. Mandatory Opt-In Requirement

You must have explicit, documented opt-in consent from every recipient before sending them any WhatsApp message through AutoSend. Opt-in must be freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous. Purchasing a contact list, scraping numbers, or assuming consent is not valid opt-in.
  • Consent must be obtained before the first message is sent, not after
  • Consent must be specific to receiving WhatsApp messages from your business
  • You must maintain records of consent and provide them to us if requested
  • Recipients must be clearly informed they can opt out at any time
  • You must immediately honour all opt-out requests and remove those numbers from future campaigns

3. Prohibited Content

You must not send messages containing:

  • Spam, bulk unsolicited messages, or messages to purchased or scraped contact lists
  • Threats, harassment, abuse, or intimidation of any kind
  • Hate speech or discrimination based on religion, caste, race, gender, or sexual orientation
  • Pornographic, sexually explicit, or obscene content
  • Content promoting violence, terrorism, or self-harm
  • Misinformation, fake news, or deliberately deceptive content
  • Malware, phishing links, viruses, or any malicious code
  • Content that infringes any copyright, trademark, or intellectual property right
  • Political propaganda, election campaigning content, or voter manipulation material

4. Prohibited Business Types

AutoSend must not be used to promote or operate the following (as per Meta's Commerce Policy):

  • Illegal goods or services of any kind
  • Drugs, narcotics, or controlled substances
  • Weapons, firearms, or ammunition
  • Gambling, betting, or lottery services (unless licensed under Indian law)
  • Multi-level marketing (MLM) or pyramid scheme operations
  • Adult content platforms or escort services
  • Counterfeit goods or fraudulent financial schemes
  • Loan shark or predatory lending operations
  • Tobacco, alcohol, or regulated pharmaceutical products without proper licensing

5. Messaging Volume & Rate Limits

  • You must respect the sending limits configured in your AutoSend settings
  • Attempting to bypass rate limits or hourly/daily caps is strictly prohibited
  • Sending patterns that trigger Meta's spam detection will result in your account being reviewed
  • You are responsible for ensuring your campaign volumes comply with Meta's tier limits for your WhatsApp Business Account

6. Message Quality Standards

  • Messages must clearly identify your business by name
  • Messages must not misrepresent your identity, products, or services
  • Messages must include clear information on how recipients can opt out
  • You are responsible for any personalisation errors caused by incorrect contact data

7. Indian Legal Compliance

All messaging activity through AutoSend must comply with:

  • Information Technology Act, 2000 — No hacking, impersonation, or unlawful interception
  • TRAI Telecom Commercial Communications Customer Preference Regulations — DND list compliance where applicable
  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 — Lawful processing of personal data
  • Consumer Protection Act, 2019 — No misleading advertisements or unfair trade practices
  • Indian Penal Code — No defamation, threats, or criminal content

8. Monitoring & Enforcement

Graphicy Media reserves the right to monitor campaign activity and complaint rates, review accounts with unusual activity, immediately suspend accounts in violation of this AUP, permanently terminate accounts with repeated or severe violations, and report violations to Meta, Razorpay, or law enforcement where required.
