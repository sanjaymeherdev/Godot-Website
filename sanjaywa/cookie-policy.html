Cookie & Storage Policy

Graphicy Media · AutoSend Platform
Effective Date: 10 May 2026
Simple summary: AutoSend does not use cookies. We use your browser's LocalStorage solely to keep you logged in between sessions. No tracking, no analytics cookies, no advertising cookies.

1. No Cookies Used

The AutoSend platform does not use HTTP cookies of any kind, including:

  • Session cookies
  • Persistent cookies
  • Third-party tracking cookies
  • Analytics cookies (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Advertising or remarketing cookies

You will never receive a cookie consent banner on AutoSend because we do not place any cookies on your device.

2. What We Use Instead — LocalStorage

AutoSend uses your browser's LocalStorage to store your session token after login. This is necessary to keep you logged in without requiring your password on every visit.

3. What LocalStorage Is

LocalStorage is a browser feature that allows websites to store small amounts of data on your device. Unlike cookies:

  • LocalStorage data is never automatically sent to any server with each request
  • LocalStorage data is never accessible by third-party websites or scripts
  • LocalStorage data is confined to your browser and device only
  • LocalStorage data persists until you manually clear it or log out

4. How to Clear Your LocalStorage

  • Via AutoSend: Click the "Logout" button in your dashboard — this automatically clears your session token
  • Via Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Clear Browsing Data → Cached images and files
  • Via Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data
  • Via Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data → Remove AutoSend entry

5. Third-Party Scripts

AutoSend does not load any third-party tracking or analytics scripts. The only external resources loaded are:

  • Google Fonts — for typography (Syne and Space Mono). Google may log a font request; no personal data is shared
  • Razorpay — only loaded when you initiate a credit purchase; subject to Razorpay's own privacy policy
  • SheetJS (xlsx.js) — loaded from cdn.sheetjs.com for Excel file import; no data is sent to SheetJS servers

6. Changes to This Policy

If we ever introduce cookies or additional storage mechanisms in the future, we will update this policy and notify all registered users by email at least 7 days in advance.
