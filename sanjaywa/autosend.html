FAQ

Common questions.

Will my WhatsApp account get banned?
+
AutoSend uses AI message variations, random human-like delays, and daily send limits to minimize ban risk. However, WhatsApp's unofficial API does carry some risk — we recommend sending to people who know you and keeping your daily sends under 200. Most users have no issues when following best practices.
Do I need a special WhatsApp number?
+
No. AutoSend works with your existing WhatsApp number. Just scan a QR code once to link your phone, and messages will be sent from your own number — the same one your customers already know and trust.
How does the credit system work?
+
1 credit = 1 message successfully delivered. Credits are only deducted when a message is sent successfully — failed messages don't cost you anything. Credits never expire, so buy in bulk and use them whenever you need.
What file formats can I import contacts from?
+
AutoSend supports Excel (.xlsx, .xls) and CSV files. It automatically detects name, phone, and message columns. Indian mobile numbers work in all formats — 10 digit, with 91 prefix, or with +91.
Can I send images, PDFs, or videos?
+
Yes. AutoSend supports images, videos, audio files, documents (PDF, Word, etc.), and stickers. Just paste the URL of your media file and it will be sent along with your text message.
Is payment secure?
+
All payments are processed by Razorpay — India's most trusted payment gateway. We support UPI, all major credit and debit cards, and netbanking. We never store your payment details.
