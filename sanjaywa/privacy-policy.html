2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Data (from our clients)

  • Full name and email address provided during registration
  • Encrypted password (never stored in plain text)
  • Platform usage data — credits purchased, messages sent, campaign logs
  • Payment transaction records via Razorpay (we do not store card or UPI details)

2.2 WhatsApp Messaging Data

  • Phone numbers of recipients that you (our client) upload to the platform
  • Message content you compose and send via the Platform
  • Delivery status and campaign logs tied to your account

2.3 Technical Data

  • Session token stored in browser LocalStorage (not cookies)
  • API request logs for debugging and abuse prevention
  • Device identifier linked to your WhatsApp Business Account
Important: We do not collect any data from WhatsApp end-users (your recipients) beyond their phone numbers as provided by you. We do not read, store, or access message replies from end-users except as necessary for delivery confirmation.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the AutoSend Platform
  • To process pay-per-use billing and maintain credit balances
  • To detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, spam, or abuse
  • To comply with Meta's WhatsApp Business Platform policies
  • To respond to your support queries and account issues
  • To ensure compliance with Indian law including the IT Act 2000 and the DPDP Act 2023

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data or your recipients' data to any third party for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All platform data is stored in Supabase with Row Level Security (RLS) enforced — each client can only access their own data
  • Session tokens are stored in your browser's LocalStorage and never transmitted to third-party servers
  • Payments are processed by Razorpay — we receive only transaction confirmation and credit-related data
  • WhatsApp messaging is routed via Meta's official WhatsApp Business API
RLS Isolation: Supabase Row Level Security ensures that no client can access another client's contacts, messages, campaigns, or settings — at the database level.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained for as long as your account is active

  • Upon account deletion request, all personal data is deleted within 30 days except legally mandated financial records

7. Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Request erasure of your personal data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (which may result in service termination)
  • Nominate a representative for data decisions in case of incapacity

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at Graphicyin@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

AutoSend is a business platform and is not intended for persons under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we become aware that a minor has registered, the account will be immediately terminated.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered clients via email at least 7 days before material changes take effect. Continued use of AutoSend after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
