FAQ

Questions before you message

The things most people wonder but don't ask.

Do I need to be technical to work with you?

Not at all. You describe the problem in plain language — "I want customers to get an automatic WhatsApp reply when they fill this form." I handle everything from there and explain what I built in plain terms.

How does "pay after it works" actually work?

I build first, you test in your own environment. Only once you confirm it does what you need, you pay via Razorpay or UPI. If it doesn't work as scoped, we keep fixing until it does — no payment until then.

What if I need changes after delivery?

3 free revisions are included after every delivery. Changes within the original scope are free. If you want something new added that wasn't in the original scope, we discuss and agree on a price first.

How fast is delivery, really?

Most standard services — WhatsApp automation, AI integrations, graphic design, automations — are same-day or next-day. Larger builds like full apps are typically 3–7 days. I'll give you an exact timeline before starting.

Will I own the code and files?

Yes. Everything I build for you is yours — the code, the server, the design files. You're not renting anything from me. You can hand it to any developer to maintain or extend after delivery.

I'm not sure what I need — can I still message you?

That's the best time to message. Describe the problem, not the solution — "I need to send 500 customers a WhatsApp message every Monday" — and I'll tell you exactly what makes sense and what it costs. Even if I can't help, I'll point you somewhere that can.

Do you work with clients outside India?

Yes. While I'm based in India and most of my clients are Indian businesses, I work remotely with clients worldwide. Pricing is always in INR — straightforward and transparent regardless of where you are.

How do I pay?

Razorpay or UPI for Indian clients. Only after you confirm the work meets the agreed scope. No advance payment, no partial upfront — the full amount only when you're satisfied.

Still not sure? Just say hi.

No commitment, no sales pitch. Tell me what's going on and I'll give you an honest answer about whether I can help — and what it would cost.
